About us

Aurora's Cake Bakery is a small business in Doornfontein that was founded by Aurora in 2021.
We specialize in designing cakes that are not only delicious but also visually stunning.and great
customer service in the area. What started as a passion for baking is slowly growsing into a
trusted bakery known for quality and creativity. We aim to make every celebration memorable
through our baked creations

Mission

To provide customers with high-quality, freshly baked cakes that meet their needs and exceed expectations.

Vision

To become a well-recognized bakery catering for large events and celebrations across the country.
